--- title: "Useful Bookmarklets" ---

Drag the following bookmarklets to your browser's bookmark folder or bar.

Career Site Publish

Used to automate publishing of a careers site. Will publish 20 files at a time until task is completed. Use on any publishing admin page. Open dev console to see status of publishing.

Career Site Pages (Sitemap)

Used to retrieve and output a spreadhseet of all pages on a careers site based on sitemap.xml. Works on both QA and Production domains.

Career Site Pages (A11y Testing)

Used to retrieve and output a spreadhseet of necessary pages on a careers site that will be used in accessibility testing. The sheet will also include links to the W3C Validator and WAVE. Works on both QA and Production domains.
