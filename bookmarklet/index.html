--- title: "Useful Bookmarklets" ---

Drag the following bookmarklets to your browser's bookmark folder or bar. When on a specifc page, click on the bookmarklet to run it.

Accessibility Testing

A11y Test (Career Site)

Used to retrieve and output a spreadhseet of necessary pages on a careers site that will be used in accessibility testing. The sheet will also include links to the W3C Validator and WAVE. Works on both QA and Production domains.

Review Alt Text (Career Site)

Used to retrieve and output a spreadhseet of of all page related images and alternative text values. This script will also indicate if the alt attribute has been ommited. Works on both QA and Production domains.

Reflow (1280 x 1024)

Used to to open the page you are viewing in a 1280 x 1024 window. Zooming into the page by 400% will then mimic the conditions needed to test reflow.

Text Spacing

Used to to mimic the minimum adjustments users may make in order to test against text spacing.

General Utilities

Career Site Publish

Used to automate publishing of a careers site. Will publish 20 files at a time until task is completed. Use on any publishing admin page. Open dev console to see status of publishing.

Sitemap (Career Site)

Used to retrieve and output a spreadhseet of all pages on a careers site based on sitemap.xml. Works on both QA and Production domains.
