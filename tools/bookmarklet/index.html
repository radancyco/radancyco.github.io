--- noindex: true title: "Useful Bookmarklets" ---

Drag the following bookmarklets to your browser's bookmark folder or bar.

Career Site Publish

Used to automate publishing of a careers site. Will publish 20 files at a time until task is completed. Use on any publishing admin page. Open dev console to see status of publishing.

Get Career Site Pages

Used to retrieve and output a spreadhseet of all pages on a careers site by reading sitemap.xml. Works on both QA and Production domains. For accessibility testing, only generate list of pages on QA.
